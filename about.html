Hi, I'm Bonnie. I am a Designer, with creative managment experience. I’m dedicated to user-centric designs which promote thoughtful, compelling interactions.

I’ve always been drawn to the technical side of the creative world. My approach to solving problems is through curiosity, ingenuity and big picture awareness to align with overall business strategy. With accessibility and product engagement in mind, I love bringing concepts to life through design in efficient and effective processes.

With a nine year background in the fashion industry with textile prints and color, I continued my education through courses in Branding and Online and Social Media Marketing. Completing a certificate in UX/UI Design and Research brought many of my interests together. Texile Design Fashion Industry

I enjoy connecting the dots between unique perspectives. Museum exhibits, curated stores, musical performances, books and travel allow me to understand how others artistically organize information and create a story or mood, as well as overall trends.

But most often I’m taking walks with my dog.
